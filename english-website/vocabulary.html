Common Collocations

Collocations are words that naturally go together.

  • make a decision, make a mistake, make progress
  • do homework, do business, do a favor
  • take a break, take care, take a look
  • have a conversation, have fun, have a problem

Tips to Build Vocabulary

  1. Read daily — books, articles, or news in English.
  2. Keep a word journal — write down new words with example sentences.
  3. Use spaced repetition — review words at increasing intervals.
  4. Learn in context — don't memorize lists; learn words through sentences.
  5. Speak and write — actively use new words to retain them.
